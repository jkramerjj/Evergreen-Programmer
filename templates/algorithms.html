Bubble Sort

A simple sorting algorithm that repeatedly steps through the list to be sorted, compares each pair of adjacent items and swaps them if they are in the wrong order.

Merge Sort

Mergesort is a divide and conquer algorithm.
Worst and Best Case performance is O(nlog(n))

Quick Sort

Quick sort is complicated to make and not always as fast as Mergesort.
Worst case is O(n^2) and Best Case performance is O(nlog(n))
