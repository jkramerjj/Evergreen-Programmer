About

I'm a computer geek at heart. I spent my youth learning on an IMB Aptiva, pounding around on Windows 95 and DOS. I have never grown tired of learning and playing around on these machines. I have a passion for software development and the countless hours that go into the profession. Which is a good thing, considering the time it take to master this craft.

I'm belong to Portland Web Design and Development meetup, I frequently attend meetups hosted by CodeFellows. (Great place btw) I'm also pursing non-profit software development to increase my skills and give back.

I began studying Web Development at Portland Community College, taking class like HTML/CSS and JavaScript. However these class lacked a challenge, the concepts were too simple and that's when I found my way into a computer science class room. From there I knew ComSci was were I needed to be, because #1 it was incredibly fascinating and #2 it was a challenge. Fastforward a few years and now I'm (almost) an Oregon State University Computer Science graduate!

Now my goal is to start a career where I can happily go to work and use my knowledge in a practical manner.

Feel free to download my resume, go to my GitHuB, or drop me a line if you'd like to chat.
